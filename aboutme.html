About Me

As a creative professional, I possess a distinctive fusion of skills and expertise that bridge the realms of Computer Information Systems (CIS) and Visual Arts.

My profound passion for the world of art compelled me to shift my academic focus from CIS to Visual Arts, setting the stage for a remarkable journey where I've harmonized and leveraged my knowledge in these two complementary domains.

Drawing inspiration from the captivating realm of street art, I embarked on a remarkable teaching journey as I shared my street art proficiency, conducting immersive street art classes at Amman Baccalaureate School for an entire year.

My journey extended further as I embraced the role of a teaching artist and muralist with Artolution.

My creative journey has not only been a local endeavor but one with a global footprint.

I had the privilege of participating in international light art festivals in Tunisia on two distinct occasions, where I showcased my exceptional talents in projection mapping and VJing.

These experiences further enriched my artistic arsenal, allowing me to explore the vibrant intersection between art and technology.

Throughout my career, I remain unwaveringly committed to infusing innovation and creativity into every project I undertake.

I'm deeply enthusiastic about the endless possibilities that arise from this harmonious blend of art and technology, and I eagerly anticipate the continued exploration of this captivating realm in my work.
